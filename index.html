The Doom Marine, referred to as the Doom Slayer in later games, is a fictional character and the protagonist in the Doom video game franchise of first-person shooters created by id Software, and its sequels and spin-off media. The Marine is a space marine dressed in green combat armor who rarely speaks onscreen.Considered a symbolic and iconic protagonist and character, the original depiction of the Doom Marine does not have a defined personality and barely portrays a predefined character. According to designer John Romero, the Marine is meant to represent the player. In 2017, Romero stated that he was the original model of the character for the cover box art.
